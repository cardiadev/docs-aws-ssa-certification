## What Are Playgrounds?

**KodeKloud Playgrounds** are AWS sandbox environments where you can practice with real services **without using your credit card or personal account**. Each session lasts up to **3 hours** and gives you access to a temporary AWS account with its own credentials.

<Callout type="info" title="Browser-based access">
Access Playgrounds at **KodeKloud.com/Playgrounds**. No installation needed — everything works directly in your web browser.
</Callout>

## Advantages vs. Personal Account

| Aspect | KodeKloud Playground | Personal AWS Account |
|---|---|---|
| **Cost** | Free (included with course) | Free Tier + risk of charges |
| **Setup** | Zero — environment ready instantly | Requires signup, credit card, IAM |
| **Billing risk** | None | High if you forget to shut down resources |
| **Duration** | **3 hours** per session | Unlimited |
| **Service coverage** | Most SAA exam services | All services |
| **Ideal for** | Quick practice while studying | Deep exploration and advanced services |

## Available Services

Playgrounds support **most services relevant to the SAA exam**:

| Category | Available Services |
|---|---|
| **Compute** | EC2, Lambda |
| **Database** | RDS, DynamoDB, DocumentDB, ElastiCache |
| **Containers** | ECS, EKS, ECR |
| **Networking** | VPC, Subnets, Security Groups, Key Pairs |
| **Storage** | S3, EBS |
| **Messaging** | SNS, SQS |
| **Security** | KMS, IAM |

<Callout type="warn" title="Services NOT available">
Some **DevOps** services are not available in Playgrounds:
- **CodePipeline**
- **CodeDeploy**

To practice with these services, you'll need your **personal AWS account**.
</Callout>

## How to Use a Playground

| Step | Action | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Start the lab** on KodeKloud | Click on the AWS Playground |
| 2 | **Receive credentials** | Account ID, IAM username, and temporary password |
| 3 | **Log in** to the AWS console | Use the provided credentials |
| 4 | **Practice freely** | Launch resources, configure services, experiment |
| 5 | **Session expires** automatically | After 3 hours everything is deleted |

<Callout type="idea" title="Tip: open a Playground for each new service">
Every time you learn a new service in the course, **open a Playground and practice it yourself**. Hands-on practice consolidates learning far faster than just watching videos. Don't wait until you finish an entire section — practice service by service.
</Callout>

## When to Use Playground vs. Personal Account?

| Situation | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Quickly practice a course service | **Playground** |
| Experiment with complex multi-service setups | **Personal account** |
| Test DevOps services (CodePipeline, CodeDeploy) | **Personal account** |
| First time using a service | **Playground** (no risk) |
| Create projects that persist beyond 3 hours | **Personal account** |

<Callout type="success" title="Summary">
- **Playgrounds** = free 3-hour AWS environments included with the course
- Support **most exam services** (EC2, RDS, S3, VPC, Lambda, etc.)
- **Not available**: CodePipeline and CodeDeploy (use your personal account for these)
- **Pro-tip**: open a Playground every time you learn a new service
- **Zero risk**: no charges, and everything is deleted when the session expires
</Callout>
