## The 4 Pillars of the Well-Architected Framework

The SAA certification evaluates your ability to design considering these **4 pillars**:

| Pillar | Question It Answers | Practical Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Security** | How do you protect your systems and data? | Encryption with KMS, IAM policies, Security Groups |
| **Reliability** | How do you survive failures? | Multi-AZ, Auto Scaling, automated backups |
| **Performance** | How do you maximize efficiency? | CloudFront, ElastiCache, instance right-sizing |
| **Cost Optimization** | How do you reduce spend? | Reserved Instances, Spot, S3 Lifecycle policies |

<Callout type="idea" title="Think like an architect">
The exam doesn't ask for CLI commands or code. It asks you to **choose the best architecture** for a given scenario. This course trains you for exactly that.
</Callout>
