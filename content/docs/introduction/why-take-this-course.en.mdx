# Why Take This Course?

Before diving in, here are the most compelling reasons to pursue this certification:

## Career Progression

From a **Jefferson Frank survey (2023)**:

> **61%** of certification holders received **more career progression opportunities** than their non-certified counterparts.

- It helps you **land a new job** or **earn promotions** in your current one.
- Not a guarantee of employment, but it becomes the deciding factor when candidates are otherwise equal.

## Higher Pay

> **73%** of certified professionals received a **pay raise** after achieving the certification.

- Most employers cover the exam cost, either partially or fully.
- Increasing your professional value typically leads to increased compensation.

## Industry Knowledge

- You'll learn the **core concepts** and most important AWS services.
- You'll develop **design abilities** specific to AWS architecture.
- By the end you'll be equipped to make architectural decisions with confidence.

## Learn to Design with AWS

The SAA certification teaches you to design against the four pillars of the **Well-Architected Framework**:

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| Security | How to make your architectures more secure |
| Reliability | How to design for high availability |
| Performance | How to maximize efficiency |
| Cost Optimization | How to reduce spend without losing functionality |
