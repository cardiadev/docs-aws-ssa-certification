# Course Introduction

## Course Objectives

The primary goal is to teach you to design on AWS across the four pillars:

- **Design Securely** — How do I improve security for EC2 instances?
- **Design for Resiliency** — How do I improve reliability for Direct Connect?
- **Design for Performance** — How do I improve performance for DynamoDB?
- **Design Cost-Efficiently** — How do I reduce costs without losing functionality?

## Who is This Course For?

- Designed for people with **Cloud Practitioner-level** knowledge.
- Assumes you have relevant production experience **or** the Cloud Practitioner certification.
- If you can't pass the Cloud Practitioner assessment, **go back and complete that course first**.

> 💡 Both Sanjeev Thiyagarajan and Michael Forrester also teach the Cloud Practitioner course.

## Course Structure

### Mega-Section 1: Meet Your Services
Covers ~100–150 AWS services across these categories:
- Networking, Storage, Compute, Database
- Application Integration, Data & ML
- Migration & Transfer, Management & Governance, Security
- **Each section** ends with a review quiz.

### Mega-Section 2: Design for X
Scenario-based questions where you apply the four design pillars to the services you've already learned:

| Section | Theme |
|---|---|
| Designing for Security | How to secure each service |
| Designing for Reliability | How to make each service resilient |
| Designing for Performance | How to optimize performance |
| Designing for Cost | How to manage and reduce costs |

Final section: an **Ultimate Design Challenge** to put everything into practice.

## Assessments

| Type | When | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Cloud Practitioner Mock Exam | At the start (optional) | Verify your baseline level |
| SAA Mock Exam (pre) | At the start (optional) | See where you stand for SAA |
| SAA Mock Exam (post) | At the end | Measure your growth |

## Recommended Study Strategies

- 📓 **Take notes** — Write each service on its own sheet of paper as you learn it.
- ⏰ **Be consistent** — 15 min/day every day > 3 hours one day and none the next.
- 🧪 **Practice with labs** — Use the course playgrounds and hands-on labs.
- 🎯 **Protect your time** — Avoid distractions and stay focused.
- 🗺 **Create mind maps** — Draw services, rewrite notes, make connections.
- ⌚ **Plan ~8 weeks** of consistent study to be ready for the exam.
