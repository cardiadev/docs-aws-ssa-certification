## Purpose

Before diving into the material, this assessment helps you **take a snapshot of your current level**. It is not a final readiness test — it's your **starting point** for measuring progress by the end of the course.

<Callout type="info" title="This assessment is diagnostic, not definitive">
The goal is to **identify areas of improvement** and focus your study on your gaps. It doesn't matter if your score is low — what matters is how much you improve after the course.
</Callout>

## Two Learner Profiles

| Profile | Experience | Take the assessment? | What to Expect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Brand New** | Coming from Cloud Practitioner or no cloud experience | **Optional** | A low score is **completely normal** |
| **Experienced** | 6+ months using AWS in production | **Highly recommended** | Reveals strong domains and specific gaps |

### If you're a beginner

<Callout type="idea" title="Don't be discouraged">
A low initial score is **exactly what's expected**. The course is designed to take you from zero to exam level. The initial assessment just gives you a number to measure your growth against.
</Callout>

### If you have experience

The assessment is especially valuable for you because:
- It identifies **which services you already master** (you can go faster through those sections)
- It reveals **specific gaps** where you need to focus
- It allows you to **optimize your study time** on what you actually need

## How to Interpret Your Result

| Score | Interpretation | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| **0–30%** | Beginner level — normal if coming from CCP | Study the entire course sequentially |
| **31–50%** | Partial knowledge — you have a foundation but lack domains | Focus extra study on areas where you struggled |
| **51–70%** | Good level — you know several services | Reinforce gaps and focus on the Design section |
| **71%+** | Advanced level — solid experience | You can move fast on known services, focus on design |

<Callout type="warn" title="Don't use this score to decide if you're exam-ready">
This is a **diagnostic** assessment, not a predictive one. Even with 71%+ you should complete the Design section since the exam focuses heavily on **architectural scenarios**.
</Callout>

## After the Assessment

| Action | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Record your score** | To compare with the final mock exam |
| **Identify weak areas** | Mark the categories where you struggled most |
| **Continue the course** | Don't dwell on the score — keep moving forward |

<Callout type="success" title="Summary">
- The assessment is **diagnostic**: measures your starting point, not your readiness
- **Beginners**: it's optional and a low score is completely normal
- **Experienced learners**: it's highly valuable for identifying specific gaps
- **Record your score** to compare with the final mock exam
- Regardless of the result — **continue with the course**
</Callout>
