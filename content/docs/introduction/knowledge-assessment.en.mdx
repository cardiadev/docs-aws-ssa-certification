# Where Are You Now? — Knowledge Assessment

## Purpose

Before diving into the material, the assessment helps you **frame your current knowledge level**. It is not a final readiness test — it is a snapshot of your starting point.

## Two Types of Learners

| Profile | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| **Brand New** (came from Cloud Practitioner or no experience) | The assessment is **optional**. A low score is completely expected — don't be discouraged |
| **Experienced** (6+ months using AWS) | The assessment is **highly valuable**. It reveals what you already know and exposes your knowledge gaps |

## What to Remember

- A low initial score is **completely normal**.
- The goal is to **identify areas of improvement** and tailor your focus — not to measure final readiness.
- Use the result as a guide, not as a measure of success or failure.
