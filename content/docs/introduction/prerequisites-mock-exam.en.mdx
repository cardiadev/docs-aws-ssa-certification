## Why This Matters

The **Solutions Architect Associate (SAA)** course assumes you already have knowledge equivalent to the **AWS Cloud Practitioner (CCP)** certification. This means you should understand basic concepts like:

| Concept | Example |
|---|---|
| **Regions and AZs** | Know what us-east-1 is, what an Availability Zone is |
| **Basic EC2** | What an instance is, instance types, AMIs |
| **Basic S3** | What a bucket is, objects, basic permissions |
| **Basic IAM** | Users, groups, policies, roles |
| **Pricing model** | Free Tier, On-Demand, Reserved |

<Callout type="warn" title="Without this foundation, the course will be very difficult">
It's **not mandatory** to hold the CCP certification, but if you don't understand these basic concepts, this course will move too fast and you'll feel lost.
</Callout>

## The Cloud Practitioner Mock Exam

The course includes a **Cloud Practitioner mock exam** as a checkpoint. It's **optional but highly recommended** if you don't hold the certification.

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Format** | CCP-style multiple choice questions |
| **Purpose** | Verify you have the necessary foundation |
| **Required** | No, but highly recommended |
| **Passing criterion** | **≥70%** |

## Interpreting Your Score

| Score | Result | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| **≥70%** | **Passed** — you have sufficient foundation | Continue with the SAA course |
| **50–69%** | **Borderline** — partial knowledge | You can continue, but consider reviewing concepts you missed |
| **&lt;50%** | **Insufficient** — needs reinforcement | Take the Cloud Practitioner course first |

<Callout type="info" title="70% is your decision line">
If you score **70% or higher**, you have enough foundation to continue. If you're below that, it's not a defeat — it simply means you need to build the foundation first with the Cloud Practitioner course.
</Callout>

## Decision Flow

| Have CCP? | Took mock exam? | Score | → Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Yes** (certification passed) | Not needed | — | Continue with SAA |
| **No**, but have AWS experience | **Yes** | ≥70% | Continue with SAA |
| **No**, but have AWS experience | **Yes** | &lt;70% | Take the CCP course first |
| **No**, no experience | **Yes** | Any | Take the CCP course first |
| **No**, no experience | **No** | — | Take the CCP course first |

</Callout>
