# Registering for the AWS SAA Exam

## Finding the Registration

1. Go to the **AWS Training and Certification** page.
2. Search for "AWS Certified Solutions Architect" and click **"Schedule an exam"**.
3. Log in using an **Amazon account dedicated to certifications** (separate from your retail account).

## Scheduling Platform

AWS uses **CertMetrics** which redirects you to **Pearson VUE** for actual exam scheduling.

## Testing Options

| Option | Advantages | Additional Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| **Testing Center (Recommended)** | Simple logistics, just show up | Government-issued photo ID |
| **Online Proctoring** | Take it from home | System test, clean space, photo ID, strict rules |

### Online Proctoring Rules
- ❌ No breaks during the exam
- ❌ Cannot stand up
- ❌ No electronics nearby
- ❌ No mumbling or lip movement
- ❌ No lip balm, food, or water allowed

## Exam Languages

- The SAA exam is available in **multiple languages**.
- With Online Proctoring you can choose your **proctor's spoken language**.

## Scheduling Tips

- Choose a time when you feel **mentally sharp and well-rested**.
- Avoid relying on caffeine or stimulants if possible.
- You can only **reschedule up to 2 times**.
- Changes must be made **at least 24 hours before** your appointment.
