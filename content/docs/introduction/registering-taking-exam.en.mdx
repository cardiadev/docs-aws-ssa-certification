## Step-by-Step Registration Process

The registration process involves **3 connected platforms**:

| Step | Platform | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **AWS Training and Certification** | Search for "AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate" and click **"Schedule an exam"** |
| 2 | **CertMetrics** | Log in with your **Amazon certification account** (separate from your retail account) |
| 3 | **Pearson VUE** | Choose date, time, modality (center or online) and complete **payment** |

<Callout type="info" title="Separate certification account">
AWS uses a **dedicated Amazon account exclusively for certifications**. It's not the same as your Amazon.com shopping account. If it's your first time, you'll create this account during the process.
</Callout>

## Modalities: Testing Center vs. Online Proctoring

| Aspect | Testing Center | Online Proctoring |
|---|---|---|
| **Location** | Pearson VUE authorized center | Your home/office |
| **Identification** | Government-issued photo ID | Government-issued photo ID |
| **Space preparation** | Not applicable (center provides it) | Clean desk, no objects |
| **System test** | Not applicable | Required before the exam |
| **Breaks** | No | No |
| **Schedule flexibility** | Center hours | 24/7 (depending on availability) |
| **Recommendation** | **Recommended** for first-timers | For those without a nearby center |

<Callout type="warn" title="Strict Online Proctoring rules">
If you choose online proctoring, these rules are **mandatory and actively monitored**:

- **No breaks** during the entire exam (130 minutes)
- **Cannot stand up** or move away from the camera
- **No electronics** nearby (phone, tablet, smartwatch)
- **No mumbling** or lip movement while reading
- **No lip balm**, food, or water allowed
- Your **desk must be completely clear**
- You must show your space with the camera before starting

Violating any of these rules can result in your **exam being voided**.
</Callout>

## Available Languages

| Element | Options |
|---|---|
| **Exam language** | English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, among others |
| **Proctor language** (online only) | You can choose your supervisor's language |
| **Recommendation** | If you're comfortable in English, **take it in English** — most AWS resources and documentation are in this language |

## Exam Scheduling Tips

| Tip | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Optimal timing** | Choose a time of day when you're **mentally sharp and well-rested** |
| **Avoid stimulants** | Don't rely on excessive caffeine — it can cause anxiety during the exam |
| **Plan with margin** | Don't schedule the exam the same day you finish the course; give yourself a few review days |
| **Arrive early** | For testing centers, arrive **30 minutes early** for check-in |

<Callout type="warn" title="Rescheduling policy">
- You can **reschedule a maximum of 2 times**
- Changes must be made **at least 24 hours in advance**
- After the second reschedule, you'll need to **cancel and pay again**
</Callout>

## Exam Cost

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| **SAA-C03 Exam** | **$150 USD** |
| **Retake (if you don't pass)** | $150 USD again |
| **Previous exam discount** | 50% off if you already have another active AWS certification |

<Callout type="idea" title="Tip: employers cover the cost">
Many employers pay for the exam as part of their professional development program. Check with your company before paying out of pocket.
</Callout>
